Burgers

A simple recipe for making plain burgers.

This one is pretty simple, but still one of my favorites.

Ingredients:

  • Angus ground beef, 93% lean
  • One packet of onion dry soup and dip mix (per pound of beef)
  • One raw egg
  • Buns (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the grill at a medium temperature (400-600)
  2. Mix beef with the onion powder and the raw egg in a mixing bowl
  3. After thorough mixing, form half pound spheres by hand
  4. Flatten the burgers into pattys, being sure to keep the center of each patty slightly thinner than the outsides, to allow for better cooking
  5. Cook each patty for roughly 15 minutes, flipping them halfway
  6. After they are finished cooking, feel free to put them on buns and dig in!

Scrambled Eggs

A simple recipe for whipping up some tasty scrambled eggs.

Scrambled eggs are an easy way to get a healthy dose of protein in with your breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • 1 tsp of olive oil
  • 1 tbsp of milk (per 2-3 eggs)
  • Salt and pepper (optional)

Steps:

  1. Put a pan on the stovetop and add the olive oil, then turn on the heat
  2. While that is heating up, add the eggs and milk to a small mixing bowl, and stir until combined
  3. Pour the mixing bowls contents into the pan, breaking them apart and stirring occasionally
  4. Once the eggs are solid and no longer shiny, take them out
  5. Add salt and pepper to your liking, and enjoy!

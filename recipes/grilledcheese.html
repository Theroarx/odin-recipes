Grilled Cheese

A simple recipe for making grilled cheese.

Grilled cheese is an easy to prepare, yet delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • Sliced cheese
  • Butter
  • Bread

Steps:

  1. Heat up a pan on the stove
  2. Lightly brush butter on one side of a piece of bread, and place it on a pan
  3. Periodically check the bottom of the bread until it is lightly browned
  4. Put the slices of cheese on the bread
  5. Lightly brush one side of the other slice of bread, place it on the cheese butter side up, and flip the sandwich.
  6. Periodically check the bottom of the bread until it is lightly browned, then take it off the pan and enjoy a grilled cheese sandwhich!
